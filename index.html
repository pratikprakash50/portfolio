The Editorial Edge

My foundation wasn't in marketing—it was in journalism. At SiliconIndia, I spent years dissecting the minds of CXOs and engineering complex publishing pipelines. When I walked into Firstock Broking, there was no content ecosystem. Just an objective.

Leveraging the editorial rigor and cross-functional leadership I honed in publishing, I didn't just write blog posts; I architected a growth engine from zero. By executing a high-volume, full-funnel strategy, we scaled to 300+ optimized articles, catapulting organic traffic from total silence to 30,000 monthly clicks and 3.4 million impressions.

Let's address the artificial elephant in the room: with AI making content production so incredibly fast, why do you still need a writer? Because while algorithms are great for speed, they often struggle with soul. I treat AI as a helpful co-pilot to multiply my throughput, while acting as the final gatekeeper to protect your brand's unique voice. When it comes to standing out, you still need a human to ensure every piece has the write stuff.

Having successfully engineered a media machine from the ground up, the blueprint is proven. Now, I am ready to deploy it against even bigger challenges.
